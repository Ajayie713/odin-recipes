Spaghetti Bolognese

Home

Description

A hearty italian pasta dish made with minced beef,tomatoes and herbs. Perfect for a family.

This recipe is simple to prepare and full of flavor, making it a go-to comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 300g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  • cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions
  • Saut√© onion and garlic until softened
  • add minced beef and cook until browned
  • stir in tomatoes and oregano, simmer for 15 mins
  • season with salt & pepper
  • serve sauce over spaghetti

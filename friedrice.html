Fried Rice

Home

Description

A delicious meal of fried rice made with minced beef, veggies and savory seasonings. Perfect for family

it can be paired with your favorite vegetables and it also goes well with grilled chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • curry and thyme
  • chicken
  • minced beef
  • mixed veggies
  • spring onion for garnish
  • garlic clove
  • onions(chopped)
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  • parboil the rice for some minutes
  • heat oil in a large pan
  • Add garlic and onions,stir-fry until fragance
  • add mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4mins until tender crisp
  • push vegetables to one sides,add eggs,scramble until cooked then mix together
  • add cooked rice,breaking up any lumps and stir-fry with vegetables
  • season with salt and pepper and every other spicies
  • garnish with spring onions and serve hot
